About Me

I'm pursuing a Ph.D.! Check out my CV/ Resume.

I am a Ph.D. student and Prime Minister's Research Fellow in the Department of Electrical Engineering at IIT Delhi. I completed my Masters from Jawaharlal Nehru University (2021) and my Bachelors in Mathematics from Jadavpur University (2018). Feel free to contact me at eez212381@iitd.ac.in.
