About Me

I'm pursuing Ph.D.! My CV/ Resume

I am a Ph.D. student and Prime Minister's Research Fellow in the Department of Electrical Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi. My primary research interest is in Graph Neural Networks and the quality of graph coarsening. I am particularly interested in effective optimization framework for graph coarsening and graph learning techniques apart from that I'm also interested in developing optimized algorithm for trading. I have worked on Un/Semi/Weakly supervised Learning, Reinforcement Learning, and Explainability.Prior to joining the Ph.D. program at IIT Delhi, I completed my Masters from Jawaharlal Nehru University, New Delhi in 2021 and my Bachelors in Mathematics from Jadavpur University, Kolkata in 2018. If you have any questions or comments, please feel free to contact me at eez212381@iitd.ac.in.
